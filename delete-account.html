UFO Hunter Account Deletion

This page explains how UFO Hunter users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your UFO Hunter account by using the account deletion request form below.

Open account deletion request form

Email Request

You may also request account deletion by emailing support:

Email: uforoom@gmail.com

Information Required

To help us identify your account, please include the email address associated with your UFO Hunter account.

If you are contacting us by email, please use the subject line: UFO Hunter Account Deletion Request

What Data May Be Deleted

When your account deletion request is processed, account-related personal data associated with your UFO Hunter account may be deleted or anonymized.

This may include account profile information, authentication-related account data, and personal information associated with your account.

User-Generated Content

UFO Hunter may include user-submitted reports, comments, images, or other content. Some content may need to be reviewed before deletion or anonymization, especially if it is part of public community features or required for safety, security, legal, or abuse-prevention purposes.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are reviewed and processed as soon as possible. We may contact you if additional verification is required.

Data Retention

Some information may be retained only when required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, abuse-prevention, or legitimate operational purposes.

Contact

For account deletion questions or support requests, please contact: uforoom@gmail.com
