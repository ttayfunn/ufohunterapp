UFO Hunter Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Overview

UFO Hunter is a UFO/UAP observation and community reporting app. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, stored, and protected when users use the app.

Information We May Collect

UFO Hunter may collect information needed to create and manage user accounts, provide app features, display user-submitted reports, and improve app reliability.

This information may include:

  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • Profile information
  • User-submitted UFO/UAP reports
  • Comments and interactions inside the app
  • Photos or videos uploaded by users
  • Selected or approximate location information related to reports
  • Device and technical information needed for app functionality
  • Notification tokens used to send app notifications

How We Use Information

We use collected information to operate UFO Hunter, provide account access, display reports and community content, send relevant notifications, respond to support requests, improve app stability, and protect the app from misuse.

User-Generated Content

UFO Hunter allows users to submit reports, comments, images, and other content. Content submitted by users may be visible to other users depending on the app feature used.

Location Information

Location information may be used when users create or view UFO/UAP reports, maps, or related observation features. Users should avoid submitting sensitive personal location details if they do not want them to be visible.

Third-Party Services

UFO Hunter may use third-party services for authentication, database storage, notifications, maps, analytics, crash reporting, or similar app functionality. These services may process data as needed to provide their functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, protect users, prevent abuse, or provide services requested by the user.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their UFO Hunter account and associated personal data.

Account deletion request form: Open account deletion form

Users may also request account deletion by emailing: uforoom@gmail.com

Children

UFO Hunter is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, or data-related requests, please contact: uforoom@gmail.com
