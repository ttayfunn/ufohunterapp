UFO Hunter Support

UFO Hunter is a UFO/UAP observation and community reporting app. This page provides support information for users who need help with the app, login, accounts, reports, privacy, or technical issues.

Contact Support

For questions, technical issues, bug reports, login problems, account support, privacy questions, or feature requests, please contact us by email.

Support Email: uforoom@gmail.com

Login and Account Help

If you cannot sign in or access your account, please contact support and include:

  • Your account email address
  • Your device model
  • Your operating system version
  • Your app version
  • A short description of the issue

Bug Reports

If you find a bug or the app does not work as expected, please email us with the steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots are helpful when available.

Privacy and Data Requests

For privacy-related questions or data requests, please contact: uforoom@gmail.com

Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their UFO Hunter account and associated personal data.

Account deletion request form: Open account deletion form

You may also request account deletion by emailing uforoom@gmail.com.
